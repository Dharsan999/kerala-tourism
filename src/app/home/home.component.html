Kerala tourism revolves around its natural beauty, diverse landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. Known as "God's Own Country", Kerala attracts visitors with its tranquil backwaters, scenic beaches, lush hill stations, and vibrant wildlife sanctuaries. The state offers opportunities for houseboat cruises, beach activities, trekking, wildlife safaris, and Ayurvedic wellness retreats. Its cultural richness is evident in traditional dance forms, festivals, and flavorful cuisine. Kerala's blend of serenity and adventure makes it a popular destination for both domestic and international travelers seeking relaxation, exploration, and cultural immersion in southern India.
